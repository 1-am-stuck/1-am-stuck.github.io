There are many steps to take to keep yourself from getting corona and spreading it to other people. Scroll down to see some of the most important.




1. Always wash your hands
You should use an antiviral handsoap for the best result

2.Always wear a mask
It is also advisable to wear two masks instead of one due to the recent starting of the second wave.

3.Stay at home and avoid going outside until absolutely necessary
it is important to quarantine yourself even if there isn't a lockdown

4.Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces
It helps your surroundings stay free from any remaining virus traces
