The covid 19 vaccine has recently been started being distributed in India. Scroll below to learn about the types of vaccine and how to sign up for it.


There are mainly three vaccines distributed in India -
1) Covisheild
2)Covaxin
3)Sputnik
The Pfizer and Moderna's coronavirus vaccines might become available in the country during the third quarter of the 2021.

How to apply to recieve the vaccine -

The vaccine is available in several Government and Private Health facilities known as CVCs(COVID Vaccination Centres)

- You can preregister online or on spot at a CVC
- You have to check wether you are elligble for that vaccine and bring all your important documents and photo id
- after recieving the vaccine you might get a mild fever but it is not something to worry about
